About OS

Archcraft is a minimal linux distribution, based on Arch Linux.

It uses lightweight apps, which makes it super minimal and fast. It only takes 200Mb of memory. User Interface is super clean. It's minimal, beautiful and gives you multiple styles to use. It has no DE installed, just two lightweight but highly configured & powerful window managers, Openbox & Bspwm. It's not only minimal and lightweight, but it's also powerful.

Desktop

This is the default desktop of Archcraft. It's Openbox window manager. Press Super+Space or Right click on the desktop to open the Menu, or just press SUPER key to run App Launcher. You can also press Ctrl+Alt+Space to open application menu with categories, if you find it suitable.

Menu & Applets

Rofi is used here for these menus and applets. Just press Super+N to open Network Menu and connect to network, Super+M to open Music Menu, Super+X to open Power menu, Super+I to open Internet Menu and Super+S to open screenshot menu.




Applications

Press Super+Return or Super+T to launch Terminal Emulator, Super+F to launch File Manager, Super+E to launch Text Editor/IDE, Super+W to launch Web Browser. Also press Ctrl+Alt+V/R/H to launch Vim, Ranger, Htop respectively. Press Ctrl+Alt+L to launch Lockscreen.

Control System

Laptop's functions keys are fully configured to use. For Ex: Press XF86Audio keys to raise / lower / mute the volume and control media playback (play / pause / next / prev), XF86MonBrightness keys to control display brightness. Press Print key to take a screenshot and more...




Manage Windows

Press Super/Alt+Tab to switch between windows, Alt+M/R to toggle Move and Resize respectively, Super+H/J/K/L to place window at corners, Super+C/Q to close window, Super+Esc to kill a window, Super+D to toggle desktop, Alt+F5/F6 to minimize, maximize window. More in OBmenu.

Workspaces & Tiling

Press Super+1/2/3/4/5 to switch workspaces, Super+Shift+1/2/3/4/5 to move active window to workspace accordingly, Super+Shift+Left/Right to move windows to next/prev workspace. Press Alt+Z/X/C and Super+Keypad keys to tile windows. See more in openbox keybinding menu.




Change Style

There are many styles available in Archcraft. To change the style, run openbox menu go to Preferences > Change Style, and click on the style you want to use. Same way you can change the Launcher and Powermenu Style and themes, just go to Change Launcher and Change Powermenu accordingly.

Small Fonts

Well, Archcraft is build on an old machine with lower resolution display and you might using a FHD+ or 4K panel, in that case, the font might feel a little small for you. Just run Openbox Menu and go to Preferences > Change Font > Globally and select a font or increase the size of current font and... Done.
